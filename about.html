Fullstack Academy

At Fullstack Academy, I’ve developed a strong foundation in modern web development through hands-on, project-based learning centered on building responsive, user-focused applications. My work has included designing clean, accessible interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as applying version control best practices with Git and GitHub to manage collaborative workflows. These projects have emphasized writing maintainable code, breaking down complex problems, and iterating quickly based on feedback.

In addition to frontend development, I’ve gained experience connecting client-side applications to backend services and APIs, strengthening my understanding of how data flows through full-stack systems. By building and deploying multi-page and single-page applications, I’ve learned how to structure projects for scalability, performance, and long-term maintainability, simulating real-world development environments and professional engineering standards.

AWS

Through my AWS studies, I’ve focused on designing and deploying secure, cloud-based architectures using core services such as S3, CloudFront, EC2, IAM, and VPC. I’ve practiced building environments that prioritize availability, performance, and security, while gaining hands-on experience with identity management, permissions, and network segmentation to protect and control access across systems.

I’ve also worked on automating deployment and infrastructure workflows using GitHub Actions and configuration-driven approaches, reinforcing best practices around CI/CD and infrastructure as code concepts. This experience has helped me understand how to balance cost optimization, monitoring, and scalability while building cloud solutions that can support production-level applications.

Consulting

In my professional role, I’ve worked as a data and systems consultant supporting enterprise SaaS implementations and large-scale data migration projects across multiple business units. I collaborate closely with business stakeholders to translate complex operational requirements into structured technical designs, building ETL-driven workflows and integration strategies that improve data quality, reliability, and system interoperability.

Alongside technical delivery, I’ve played a key role in documentation, validation, and cross-team communication to ensure solutions are aligned with both business goals and technical constraints. This work has strengthened my ability to bridge the gap between engineering teams and non-technical partners, while delivering scalable, well-governed systems that support long-term organizational growth.
